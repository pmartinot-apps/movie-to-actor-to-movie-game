# Privacy Policy – Movie to Actor to Movie Game

**Last updated: February 28, 2026**

A game where you guess an actor from a movie, then another movie from that actor infinitely!

This Privacy Policy describes how Movie to Actor to Movie Game ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

**Movie to Actor to Movie Game does not collect any personal data.**

We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

- No account or registration is required
- No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted
- No advertising identifiers are collected
- No analytics or crash reporting services are used

---

## 2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores game history (scores, journey steps, game statistics) exclusively on your device using local storage. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is never transmitted to us or any third party
- Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App
- Can be cleared at any time from within the App

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App fetches movie and actor data from the [TMDb API](https://www.themoviedb.org/) (The Movie Database) to display film and cast information during gameplay.

- Only generic search queries (movie titles, actor names) are sent to the TMDb API
- No personal data or device identifiers are included in these requests
- TMDb's own privacy policy applies: [themoviedb.org/privacy-policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy)

No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking services are integrated into this App.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, there is no additional risk for younger users.

---

## 5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk. Game data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own security mechanisms.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no data about you to access, correct, or delete on our end. All locally stored game data can be removed by uninstalling the App.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
